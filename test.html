The Ladder of Change

T1

Quick Wins

Immediate relief that builds credibility fast.

T1.1
Energy Savings & Lower Bills

Bigger rebates, simpler local programs, community solar‚Äîutility bills down.

Open blueprint
Vehicle: Minibus or suspension. Pass: pair with fraud controls & dashboards. Fail: pilot via grant criteria.
  • Scenario A: bundle w/ energy title.
  • Scenario B: House-pass; write pilots into report language.
  • KPIs: households served, avg. monthly savings, M&V compliance.
T1.2
Fair Food & Grocery Competition

Stop junk fees and exclusionary contracts so groceries stay fair and affordable.

Open blueprint
Vehicle: Suspension or Commerce title. Pass: ban specific fee types; empower FTC/DOJ. Fail: transparency + AG grants.
  • Scenario A: standalone first; add private right of action later.
  • Scenario B: House-pass; slot AG enforcement grants in approps.
  • KPIs: fee elimination rate, small-grocer margins, consumer complaint drop.
T1.3
Good Jobs & Skills

Apprenticeships up, red tape down, training tied to local hiring.

Open blueprint
Vehicle: Labor/HHS title. Pass: outcome-based funding. Fail: WIOA guidance + bonus criteria.
  • Scenario A: pair with employer compacts.
  • Scenario B: pilots + reporting via committee report.
  • KPIs: placements, wage gains, time-to-hire.
T2

Relief & Stability

Deeper household relief with strong guardrails.

T2.1
Child & Dependent Care

Lower copays, more seats, stable staffing.

Open blueprint
Vehicle: HHS title. Pass: cap copays on sliding scale. Fail: supply grants first.
  • A: move with offsets from Tax Fairness.
  • B: stabilization grants via report language.
  • KPIs: seats added, median copay %, provider retention.
T2.2
Permitting Modernization

Digitize & speed approvals; add homes near jobs & transit.

Open blueprint
Vehicle: THUD title. Pass: launch grants; publish queue-time targets. Fail: grants-only pilot.
  • A: waterfall into 3% Mortgage within 10 days.
  • B: DOT/HUD demo language.
  • KPIs: median days-to-permit, units added near transit.
T2.3
First-Time Homebuyer 3% Mortgage

~3% fixed for first-timers with fraud analytics and guardrails.

Open blueprint
Vehicle: Banking/HUD. Pass: staged rollout. Fail: 5-metro pilot, first-gen buyers.
  • A: file within 10 days of permitting passage.
  • B: FHFA pilot directive.
  • KPIs: defaults, time-to-close, % first-gen buyers.
T3

Accountability

Fair rules and real enforcement.

T3.1
Tax Fairness

15% book minimum, close offshoring loopholes, expand CTC/EITC.

Open blueprint
Vehicle: Finance/WTM. Pass: pair with CSR to fund T2 programs. Fail: transparency first.
  • A: offsets cover childcare/permitting grants.
  • B: country-by-country + enforcement only.
  • KPIs: revenue scored, audit yield, offshoring gap closed.
T3.2
Corporate Responsibility (CSR)

Surcharges for wage theft/extreme pay gaps; credits for good actors.

Open blueprint
Vehicle: Finance/WTM. Pass: move with Tax Fairness. Fail: start with repeat-offender penalties + reporting.
  • A: paired calendar slot.
  • B: OSHA/EPA lists tied to tax penalties.
  • KPIs: violations reduced, pay-ratio movement, credits uptake.
T4

System Reform

Clean money in politics and transparent votes.

T4.1
Clean Campaigns & Elections

Ban corporate PAC $, near-real-time disclosure, voucher pilots.

Open blueprint
Vehicle: FSGG. Pass: full title. Fail: disclosure + voucher pilots only.
  • A: couple with Vote Justification.
  • B: vendor-disclosure rider; state-match pilots.
  • KPIs: small-donor share, disclosure lag, voucher redemption.
T4.2
Congressional Vote Justification

Single-subject discipline + standardized vote explanations.

Open blueprint
Vehicle: rules + authorizing. Pass: House/Senate rules + statute. Fail: House-only rules + public scorecards.
  • A: pair with Clean Campaigns.
  • B: committee report requirements for vote memos.
  • KPIs: % bills single-subject, memo publication rate.
T5

Long-Term Guarantees

Big lifts once trust is earned.

T5.1
Universal Healthcare

Auto-coverage with a national floor; drug costs down; parity enforced.

Open blueprint
Vehicle: authorizing multi-year. Pass: phase-in; strong transition. Fail: negotiation + auto-enroll pilots.
  • A: move once T1-T3 wins banked.
  • B: CMMI demos + state waivers.
  • KPIs: uninsured rate, OOP costs, drug price index.
T5.2
Social Security Solvency

End taxes on most benefits; fair top-end contributions; protect checks.

Open blueprint
Vehicle: authorizing. Pass: lift cap; firewall fund. Fail: commission + actuarial triggers.
  • A: pair with revenue fairness narrative.
  • B: bipartisan solvency working group.
  • KPIs: 75-yr actuarial balance, benefit tax relief reach.
