Post flyers in your neighborhood

One of the fastest ways to grow is simple: visible flyers on community mailboxes and neighborhood boards. We can send an approved flyer, or a volunteer can drop off ready-to-post flyers at a location you choose.

Print at home

We’ll send an approved flyer PDF. Print and post where it’s allowed.

  • Best spots: community mailboxes, local boards, coffee shops (with permission)
  • Optional: laminate for weather resistance
  • We’ll share simple posting guidelines
Request flyer PDF

Tip: laminating makes outdoor posting last longer, especially near community mailbox clusters.
