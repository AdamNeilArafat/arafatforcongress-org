Endorsements

This page provides a transparent view of current endorsement status for the campaign. We clearly separate what is confirmed, what is pending confirmation, and what is in process.

Last updated: March 3, 2026

Status definitions

Each endorsement is listed in only one category so supporters can quickly understand its verification status.

Confirmed

Formally granted by the organization through a public listing, official communication, or direct campaign verification.

Pending confirmation

Informally communicated support that has not yet been formally verified by the endorsing organization.

In process

Outreach or review conversations are underway, but no endorsement commitment has been made.
