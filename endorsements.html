Endorsements

Who is standing with WA-10.

This campaign is people-funded and people-built. Endorsements are earned by listening to workers, families, and community leaders across Pierce, Thurston, and Mason counties, not by writing checks to party committees.

Full transparency on endorsement status This page shows the accurate status of every endorsement: confirmed, pending confirmation, and organizations where outreach is in progress. We do not list endorsements we have not received.

Citizens Impeachment

Citizens Impeachment has designated Adam Arafat a Courage Candidate, recognizing his commitment to holding elected officials accountable to the people and restoring integrity to public service. This recognition is reserved for candidates who demonstrate a willingness to stand for accountability regardless of political cost.
