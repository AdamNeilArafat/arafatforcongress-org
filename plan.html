The Plan

Lower costs. Honest government. Real results.

No corporate PAC money, no strings, just people building power together. This is not about ideology. It is overdue modernization that protects our way of life and strengthens the working and middle class that keeps this country running.

Top Priorities

Clear commitments you can hold me to. Backed by real legislation.

Lower Costs

  • Crack down on price fixing and corporate collusion that raise the cost of living.
  • Ban junk fees across all services and industries.
  • Expand affordable housing and childcare near where families live and work.

Healthcare You Can Use

  • Protect and strengthen Medicare and the ACA and close coverage gaps.
  • Enforce the No Surprises Act and expand caps on out-of-pocket costs.
  • Fund local mental health and addiction care so help is close by.
  • Begin the path toward universal healthcare that guarantees coverage for every American.

The United States is the only one of 38 developed countries without universal healthcare. We spend about $12,555 per person each year while millions still skip care because they cannot afford it.

End Pay-to-Play Politics

  • No corporate PAC money, ever.
  • Ban stock trading in Congress.
  • Protect whistleblowers and Inspectors General.

What I'll Fight For

Real work that moves WA-10 forward.

Housing You Can Afford

  • Streamline zoning and permits for affordable and workforce housing.
  • Tie federal funds to fair renter protections and eviction due process.
  • Help first-time buyers with real down-payment support so working families can build wealth.
  • Provide tax credits for ADUs and small starter home conversions.

Strong Public Schools

  • Boost Title I and IDEA so districts can lower class sizes and expand special education.
  • Modern classrooms with safe air and water.
  • Career and technical programs linked to local jobs.

Good Jobs and Unions

  • Expand apprenticeships and skills training.
  • Protect the right to organize (H.R. 20, PRO Act).
  • Increase capital access for small businesses.

Safer Communities

  • Invest in youth programs and violence prevention.
  • Transparency and accountability tied to federal dollars.
  • Expand mental health crisis response across WA-10.
  • Increase social safety programs proven to reduce crime.

Clean Air and Water

  • Modernize the grid and speed clean energy development.
  • Protect rivers, coasts, and forests across WA-10.
  • Fund home and small business efficiency programs.

Protect Rights and Freedom

  • Protect access to abortion and contraception.
  • Restore voting rights and fair maps.
  • Defend LGBTQ+ Americans from discrimination.

I'm running to fight for people, not the powerful. If you're tired of political games and want someone who understands what it costs to raise a family and make ends meet, this campaign is for you.

If you're working class and tired of politicians who talk big but never fight for you, vote for someone who will.

Adam Arafat, WA-10

Citizens serving citizens. That's the job.
