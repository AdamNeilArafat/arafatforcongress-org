A plan for WA-10 families that can be measured.

From JBLM households to Port of Tacoma jobs, Olympia workers, I-5 commuters, and South Tacoma/Spanaway neighborhoods, people need relief and honest follow-through.

  • No corporate PAC money
  • Transparent and specific: bill numbers, votes, and receipts
  • Working-family standard: action, not press releases

Four pillars

1) Lower the cost squeeze

  • Housing supply near jobs/transit
  • Target rent and utility pressure

Rationale: families can’t plan for the future if every month is a scramble.

2) Affordable healthcare

  • Lower out-of-pocket costs
  • Protect access and continuity of care

Rationale: delayed care is expensive care, and working people pay twice.

3) Worker power and wages

  • Back collective bargaining
  • Raise standards for pay and leave

Rationale: productivity gains should show up in paychecks, not just corporate reports.

4) Accountability government

  • Public vote tracker and bill tracker
  • Quarterly transparency updates

Rationale: voters deserve receipts, timelines, and course-corrections.
