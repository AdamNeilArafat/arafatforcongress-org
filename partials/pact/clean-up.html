Clean Up Politics

  • Ban corporate PAC cash-outs and lobbyist bundling; tighten coordination rules Clean Campaigns & Elections Bill
  • Shift power to voters with small-dollar vouchers and public match programs Voter Power Act
  • Require near real-time campaign finance reporting the public can actually use Transparent Reporting Act
  • Expose dark money with clear disclosure of who is funding committees and ads True Source Disclosure Bill
  • Modernize the FEC so violations are acted on fast and penalties stick FEC Modernization Act
  • Require plain-language vote justifications for key bills so constituents see the why Congressional Vote Justification Bill
