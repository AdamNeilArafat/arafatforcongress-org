Meet Adam

20 years serving America. I served in the U.S. Army, building aviation maintenance programs and managing airfield operations worldwide. Service taught me discipline, accountability, and to put people first.

Today I work in public service with the county, delivering infrastructure that hundreds of thousands rely on. My family and I live in Spanaway—this is our home.

Values

I refuse corporate PAC money and answer only to the people of WA-10. I will protect constitutional rights and fight for Medicare for All, lower costs and more housing, and honest, people-powered government.
