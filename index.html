Why It Matters

Our country is at a crossroads. The old guard has had decades to lead, but clinging to the status quo has left too many families struggling. It’s time to pass the torch to the next generation of leaders so our nation can move forward and begin to heal.

What the right has branded as “progressive” policies are only progressive if you measure them against a broken system stuck in the past. When you measure against the rest of the world, these aren’t radical ideas at all — they’re simply us trying to catch up. Healthcare for all, affordable living, and ending corruption are not leaps ahead, they are the basics modern nations already provide their people. America shouldn’t lag behind — we should lead.

Adam believes change starts with leaders grounded in our communities — working-class Americans who aren’t beholden to foreign interests, corporate sponsors, or lobbyists. It’s time to stop waiting and start building a future led by integrity, optimism, and results.
