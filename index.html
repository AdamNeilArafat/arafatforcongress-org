Power to the People, Not the PACs

Leaders should answer to voters, not donors. Outside money drowns out local voices. We are building a campaign where neighbors set the agenda and accountability never goes on mute.

Lower Costs

  • Stop price gouging on everyday essentials.
  • Strengthen safety nets that working families can actually use.
  • Raise wages and protect households under $400K from new taxes.

What I’ll do: Push federal action that lowers household costs while asking the wealthy to pay their fair share.

Universal Healthcare

  • Guarantee coverage for everyone.
  • Let people keep the doctors they trust.
  • Cut billing games that trap families in paperwork and debt.

What I’ll do: Fight for universal healthcare with transparent pricing and less bureaucracy.

Homes People Can Afford

  • Invest in housing where demand is highest.
  • Expand first-time buyer access to affordable financing.
  • Support stable pathways to homeownership.

What I’ll do: Back housing policies that expand supply and make mortgages more affordable for first-time buyers.

Good Jobs

  • Expand apprenticeships and union career pathways.
  • Remove unnecessary certification barriers.
  • Grow trade and tech opportunities that pay family-supporting wages.

What I’ll do: Invest in workforce programs that connect local workers to good jobs in WA-10.

About Adam

Adam Arafat is a veteran, public servant, and program manager who understands what rising costs mean for working families. He has spent more than two decades serving community and country, and he is running to bring that same accountability to Congress. His campaign is focused on practical results: lower costs, stronger healthcare, and good jobs that let people stay and grow in WA-10. This campaign is about listening first and governing with transparency.
