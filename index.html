Why it matters

Too many in Congress work for PACs and donors, not the people they represent. In 2024, four out of five dollars backing the incumbent came from outside WA-10. Nearly half came from PACs. That’s not independence—that’s influence.

On paper, 48 bills were sponsored. Only 4 became law. Most were routine or ceremonial. When it comes to the hard fights—healthcare, housing, and costs—WA-10 has lacked visible leadership.

I refuse corporate PAC money. I will fight for Medicare for All, lower costs and more housing, and honest government. I live here, I’ve served, and I’m ready to deliver.

Meet Adam

20 years serving America. Now building WA-10’s future. I served in the U.S. Army, building aviation maintenance programs and managing airfield operations worldwide. Service taught me discipline, accountability, and to put people first.

Today I work in public service with the county, delivering infrastructure that hundreds of thousands rely on. My family and I live in Spanaway—this is our home.

Values

I refuse corporate PAC money and outside influence. I answer only to the people of Washington’s 10th District. I will protect constitutional rights, including the Second Amendment, because freedom and quality of life go together.

The same discipline that guided me in uniform guides my fight now—for Medicare for All, lower costs and more housing, and honest, people-powered government.
