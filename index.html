Meet Adam

Adam Arafat is a veteran, public servant, and program manager. He knows the burden of balancing work, family, and rising costs. With more than 20 years of service to community and country, he brings real world experience, not political privilege. He is running to restore accountability, lower costs, and fight for every family in WA-10.

Power to the People, Not the PACs

Leaders should answer to voters, not donors. Outside money drowns out local voices. We are building a campaign where neighbors set the agenda and accountability is non negotiable.

Lower Costs

Stop price gouging. Subsidize essentials like food. Strengthen safety nets that work. Raise the minimum wage. Tax the wealthy, not working families. No new taxes under $400K.

Universal Healthcare

Coverage for everyone. Keep your doctor. Cut the paperwork.

Homes People Can Afford

Subsidize housing in high need areas. Create low interest mortgages under 3 percent for first time buyers.

Good Jobs

More apprenticeships and union jobs. Remove pointless certification hurdles. Focus on trades and real career paths.
