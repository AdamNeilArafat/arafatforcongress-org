Meet Adam

Adam Arafat is a veteran, public servant, and lifelong advocate for working families. He understands the challenges of balancing work, family, and rising costs as he deals with these issues daily. With over 20 years of service to community and country, Adam brings real world experience, not political privilege. He is running to restore accountability, lower costs, and fight for every family in WA-10.

Power to the People, Not the PACs

Leaders should answer to voters, not donors. Outside money drowns out local voices. We are building a campaign where neighbors set the agenda and accountability is non negotiable.

End the Pay to Play Culture

Ban corporate PAC money and open every book. Transparency must be the standard, not the slogan.

Lower Costs for Families

Results, Not Press Releases

Fewer speeches, more wins on healthcare, housing, childcare, schools, and jobs in WA-10.
