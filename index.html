Why WA-10 Is Winnable

This is a competitive district. Donors and endorsers need to see the math. Here it is.

The district is shifting. WA-10 registration trended 4 points more Democratic between 2020 and 2024. Pierce County added 22,000 net new registered voters — majority under 40.

The incumbent's margins are soft. In 2022, Strickland won with 59% — down from 63% in 2020. Challenger-favorable conditions in 2026 (off-year, high incumbent disapproval) compress that further.

JBLM is a swing constituency. Military families split tickets at higher rates than civilian households. Adam's service background and district residency give him credibility in communities where Strickland has underperformed.

The issues favor a challenger. Housing costs, healthcare gaps, and wage stagnation are most acute in Pierce County — the core of WA-10. Strickland's record on progressive bills creates a clear, documentable contrast that doesn't require voters to take anything on faith.

Small-dollar infrastructure is ready. The campaign is ActBlue-enabled, social-media active, and building volunteer infrastructure. A labor endorsement or WCV endorsement would provide phone bank and canvass infrastructure that no dollar amount can replicate.

The playbook exists. Progressive challengers have won comparable seats in WA state in 2020 and 2022 by out-organizing on local economic issues. This campaign is built on the same model.

The Standard in Congress

If you claim to support working-family priorities, you put your name on them and you use leverage to move them. Anything else is a statement, not a result.

The minimum

Co-sponsor the bills you claim to support and show up when the vote matters.

The job

Force action when leadership would rather delay. That is what urgency looks like in DC.

Fighting means using leverage.

  • Attach priorities to must-pass legislation.
  • Make them non-negotiable conditions.
  • Escalate when they stall.
  • Force votes instead of accepting delay.

I will co-sponsor the working-family bills this district supports and fight to move them. If you want receipts, I will publish them.
