Meet Adam Neil Arafat

20 years serving America. Now building WA-10's future.

Adam served this country in the U.S. Army, building aviation maintenance programs and managing airfield operations across the globe. His service instilled discipline, accountability, and the importance of putting people before politics.

He has built infrastructure that kept communities moving. Now he is building futures here at home. In Spanaway, Adam and his family are raising their children in Washington's 10th District. Like many families here, they feel the pressure of rising costs and healthcare uncertainty and they want safe, thriving neighborhoods where kids can grow and succeed.

Today, Adam works in public service with the county, building and overseeing infrastructure programs that provide essential services to hundreds of thousands of people. He brings the same focus on results, efficiency, and transparency that he will take to Congress.

Unity means building a district where no pocket is left behind. Working families, veterans, and independent voices move forward together. He rejects corporate PAC money, lobbyists, and foreign influence, answering only to the people of WA-10.

Trust is built, not bought. That is the foundation of this people-powered campaign and how we will deliver healthcare for all, lower the cost of living, and end corruption.
