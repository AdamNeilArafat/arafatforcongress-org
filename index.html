Meet Adam

20 years serving America. Now building WA-10's future. Adam served in the U.S. Army, building aviation maintenance programs and managing airfield operations worldwide. His service instilled discipline, accountability, and the importance of putting people first.

Today, Adam works in public service with the county, building and overseeing infrastructure programs that provide essential services to hundreds of thousands of people. In Spanaway, he and his family are raising their children right here in WA-10.

He rejects corporate PAC money, lobbyists, and foreign influence, answering only to the people of WA-10. Trust is built, not bought. That is the foundation of this people-powered campaign to deliver healthcare for all, lower costs, and end corruption.
