Meet Adam

Adam Arafat is a veteran, public servant, and program manager who knows the weight of balancing work, family, and rising costs. With more than two decades serving community and country, he brings real-world experience, not political privilege. He is running to restore accountability, lower costs, and fight for every family in WA-10.

Power to the People, Not the PACs

Leaders should answer to voters, not donors. Outside money drowns out local voices. We are building a campaign where neighbors set the agenda and accountability never goes on mute.

You shouldn’t need a lobbyist to be heard by your representative.

Lower Costs

Stop price gouging, subsidize essentials, strengthen safety nets that work, and raise the minimum wage. Tax the wealthy, not working families, and promise no new taxes under $400K.

Universal Healthcare

Guarantee coverage for everyone, keep the doctors you trust, and cut the billing games that bury families in paperwork.

Homes People Can Afford

Invest in housing where demand is highest and create low-interest mortgages under three percent so first-time buyers can plant roots.

Good Jobs

Expand apprenticeships and union careers, clear pointless certification hurdles, and focus on trades and tech pathways that actually pay.
