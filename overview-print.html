Evergreen Pact — Static Overview for 2027

Two ladders. Each step helps working people without new taxes on working families and adds checks on executive power.

Path A: House and Senate

Tier 0, Guardrails first (January to June 2027)
  • Implementation Safeguards
  • Communications and Seals Integrity
  • Defense emergency approval to spend pilot
  • Continuity expansion, government wide
Tier 1, Quick wins (January to February 2027)
  • Energy Savings and Lower Bills
  • Fair Food and Grocery Competition
  • Good Jobs and Skills
Tier 2, Relief and stability (February to April 2027)
  • Child and Dependent Care
  • Housing Permitting Modernization
  • First time homebuyer three percent mortgage
Tier 3, Fairness (March to June 2027)
  • Tax Fairness
  • Corporate Responsibility
Tier 4, System reform (June to September 2027)
  • Clean Campaigns and Elections
  • Congressional Vote Justification
Tier 5, Long term guarantees (Late 2027 and beyond)
  • Universal Healthcare
  • Social Security solvency
