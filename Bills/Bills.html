Corporate minimums and anti‑avoidance; no new taxes on working families.

Fair Food & Grocery Antitrust enforcement; curb MFNs & junk fees; supplier/fee transparency. Fairer markets; lower grocery prices. Litigation risk; retailer resistance. Enforcement‑driven; modest admin costs.
Energy Savings & Lower Bills Bigger rebates for weatherization/heat pumps; community solar; simplify LIHEAP. Lower monthly bills; cleaner, safer homes. Program delivery capacity; contractor availability. Rebate funding + utility/community partnerships.
First‑Time Homebuyer 3% Federally backed ~3% mortgages for first‑time buyers with price caps and oversight. Improves affordability; builds middle‑class wealth. Guard against price inflation; strong underwriting needed. Program fees, reallocations, existing housing tools; budget‑neutral design.
Clean Campaigns & Elections Ban corporate PAC money; near real‑time disclosure; pilot voter vouchers. Transparency; people‑powered campaigns. Legal challenges; implementation complexity. Administrative; leverages existing agencies.
Vote Justification Standardized, factual vote explanations; single‑subject rule. Radical transparency; better deliberation. Member workload; risk of boilerplate. Administrative; within existing processes.
Universal Healthcare Automatic enrollment; national benefits floor; lower drug costs; mental health parity. Universal coverage; lower per‑capita spend over time. Transition planning; provider capacity. Fair top‑end contributions + savings; no new taxes on working families.
Social Security Solvency End benefit taxes for most; contributions resume above $400k; add investment/estate support. Protects checks; closes solvency gap. High‑earner contributions; inter‑agency coordination. Dedicated receipts to trust funds.
