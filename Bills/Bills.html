What It Is

The Evergreen Pact is a strategically ordered set of bill blueprints designed to prove reform is not random, it is structured. Each bill is a rung on a ladder of change. Early wins make harder reforms possible, and every success stacks momentum toward long-term guarantees for working families.

Strategic Sequencing: The Ladder of Change

Tier 1: Quick Wins

  1. Energy Savings & Lower Bills
    • Bigger rebates and simpler local programs that cut utility bills with weatherization, efficient appliances, and community solar.
  2. Fair Food & Grocery Competition
    • Cracks down on junk fees and anti competitive contracts so grocery markets are fair and families pay less at checkout.
  3. Good Jobs & Skills
    • Right sizes licensing, expands apprenticeships, and aligns training with local employers so people get into good jobs faster.

Broad bipartisan appeal with immediate relief that builds credibility fast.

Tier 2: Relief & Stability

  1. Affordable Child & Dependent Care
    • Lowers childcare costs and expands seat supply while supporting providers so parents can work and kids thrive.
  2. Housing Supply & Permitting Modernization
    • Speeds up digital permitting, adds homes near jobs and transit, and supports modular building to lower costs.
  3. First Time Homebuyer 3% Mortgage
    • Offers federally backed around 3 percent fixed mortgages for first time buyers with strong guardrails and oversight.

Deeper household relief and structural fixes powered by trust earned in Tier 1.

Tier 3: Accountability

  1. Tax Fairness
    • Sets a 15 percent book profit minimum, closes offshoring loopholes, expands CTC and EITC, and improves transparency without new taxes on working families.
  2. Corporate Responsibility (CSR)
    • Applies surtaxes tied to wage theft, extreme pay gaps, and repeat OSHA or EPA violations and rewards good actors with credits.

Cost of living wins make it harder for lobbyists to block fairness and responsibility.

Tier 4: System Reform

  1. Clean Campaigns & Elections
    • Bans corporate PAC money, speeds disclosure to near real time, and pilots voter vouchers so campaigns answer to people.
  2. Congressional Vote Justification
    • Requires standardized factual explanations for key votes and enforces a single subject rule for clearer lawmaking.

Change the rules after credibility and pressure are built by earlier tiers.

Tier 5: Long Term Guarantees

  1. Universal Healthcare
    • Delivers automatic coverage with a national benefits floor, lower drug costs, and mental health parity.
  2. Social Security Solvency
    • Ends benefit taxes for most retirees and restores fair top end contributions so checks stay secure for the long haul.

The biggest lifts come last and become realistic after earlier wins are banked.
