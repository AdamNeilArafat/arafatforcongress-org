Senate Rule XIV - Calendar Placement

How to have a measure read twice and placed on the calendar without committee referral, with template floor language and coordination notes.

Guidance

  • Use when rapid floor availability is necessary or to preserve a live vehicle.
  • Coordinate with right of first recognition and floor time. Consider filling the tree on heavy days.
  • Pair with a House message or a shell vehicle for later amendment.

Template Floor Language

Mr. President, I ask that the bill be read a first time.
Clerk reads title.
I now ask for a second reading and that the bill be placed on the calendar.
Objection heard or waived per Rule XIV.
So ordered.

Notes

Use Rule XIV to maintain flexibility. When ready, proceed to a motion to proceed, secure debate time, and manage amendments.
