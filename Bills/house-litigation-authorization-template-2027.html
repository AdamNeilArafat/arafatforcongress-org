House Litigation Authorization. Clear standards, transparent steps, and quick enforcement with public results.

At a Glance

  • Cost: Neutral overall with targeted administrative costs offset by savings and reduced waste.
  • Impact: Improves fairness, transparency, and service delivery for the public.
  • Who is covered: Agencies, regulated parties, and the public who rely on these services.
  • Safeguards: Life safety carve outs, sunsets and reviews, and court enforcement lanes.

Why it matters

This bill sets clear standards. It reduces gamesmanship. It gives the public a fair, timely, and enforceable process.

Will this slow down urgent work?

No. Urgent safety work and court ordered compliance continue with narrow and renewable certifications.

Is there new bureaucracy?

No. The approach is simple. It uses short certifications and public notice backed by independent checks.

Does this change taxes?

No. The focus is on standards, fairness, and better execution. Any costs are covered by savings and recovery of waste.

Resolution Text
RESOLUTION
Authorizing the Office of General Counsel of the House of Representatives to appear in any civil action, and to file amicus briefs or seek injunctive relief, to enforce publication, authentication, and appropriations conditions during any impeachment covered period.

Resolved, That the Office of General Counsel of the House of Representatives is authorized to represent the House in any civil action, including seeking temporary restraining orders and preliminary injunctions, to enforce compliance with publication and authentication requirements, the use-of-funds condition, and related safeguards for executive documents and actions first initiated during a covered period, as defined in House rules or statute.
Sec. 2. The Office of General Counsel may file briefs amicus curiae or intervene, as appropriate.
Sec. 3. The Office of General Counsel shall report to the House on actions taken under this resolution within 30 days and quarterly thereafter while any such action remains pending.
