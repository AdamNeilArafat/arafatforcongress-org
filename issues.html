Healthcare

Healthcare should be a basic human right. The United States is the only country among the 38-member Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development (OECD) that does not offer universal healthcare, despite being the wealthiest. I support a public, single-payer system like Medicare for All so families don’t have to choose between care and bankruptcy.

Housing

One way the federal government can help is by offering first-time homebuyers a special low-interest mortgage program. We also need rent relief and to tax real estate speculation that drives up costs for working families.

Fair Taxation

I support closing loopholes and taxing the ultra-wealthy fairly. We need a system where Amazon doesn’t pay zero in taxes while everyday Americans struggle. When big corporations pay their fair share, we all benefit.

Lower Cost of Living

Groceries, gas, and rent have all gone up. I support smart subsidies to American farmers, energy independence, and strong labor protections to help reduce prices and keep families stable.

Reproductive Freedom

I support the right to choose. Government should never interfere in personal medical decisions. We need to protect access to abortion care, contraception, and comprehensive reproductive health services for all.

Climate Action

Climate change is the biggest threat to our future. I support ending fossil fuel subsidies and investing in clean energy infrastructure, green jobs, and transit systems that reduce emissions while creating opportunity.

Workers First

I support strong unions, fair wages, paid family leave, and protections for gig and service workers. We need to restore the balance of power to working people, not corporate boards.

Transparent Government

No more backroom deals. I support banning corporate PACs and special interest money, and instead pushing for a public campaign financing system, like Seattle’s Democracy Voucher Program, to level the playing field.

Strengthen Democracy

I support expanding voting rights, passing the John Lewis Voting Rights Act, securing elections, and ending gerrymandering so every vote counts and every voice is heard.

Veterans & Military Families

As a 20-year Army veteran, I know the sacrifices made by military families. I support improving VA healthcare, housing support, mental health access, and transition services that honor our commitments to those who served.
