Issues

These are the priorities Adam will champion in Congress.

Lower Cost of Living

Everyone deserves an affordable place to live and enough to eat. Adam will fight for affordable housing, lower food costs, fair wages and a secure social safety net so families can thrive. He will champion policies that cap rent increases and expand housing supply while raising the minimum wage and protecting Social Security.

Universal Healthcare

Healthcare is a human right. Adam supports universal healthcare where every medical decision is made between patients and doctors—not politicians or insurance companies. He will work to expand Medicare, lower drug prices and ensure mental health services are available to everyone.

Fair Taxation

For too long the ultra‑rich and big corporations have gamed the system. Adam will close loopholes and make sure the wealthy and corporations pay their fair share so we can invest in communities. He will push for progressive taxes and end unfair corporate subsidies while protecting middle‑class families.

No Special Interests & Lobbyists

Our campaign is powered by people, not lobbyists. Adam will refuse special‑interest money and put constituents first—no strings attached. He will champion campaign finance reform and fight to overturn Citizens United so elections are decided by voters, not donors.

Corporate Accountability

Corporations must pay their workers fairly and follow the rules. Adam will hold corporations accountable, end sweetheart deals and protect workers’ rights. He will crack down on wage theft, strengthen unions and ensure polluters pay for the damage they cause.

Support Farmers & Trade

Our farmers feed the nation and deserve policies that sustain them. Adam will champion fair trade, invest in local agriculture and protect farmers from unfair corporate practices. He will work to improve crop insurance, expand markets for small producers and oppose trade deals that hurt workers.

Climate Action Now

Our planet can’t wait. Adam will push for clean energy, cut polluter handouts and invest in green jobs to build a sustainable future for our kids. He will support a Green New Deal, invest in public transit and protect our natural resources.
