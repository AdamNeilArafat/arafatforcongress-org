Where I Stand

Clear commitments. Federal action. Real results. These are the fights I’ll take on in Congress. Each pledge is backed by national lawmaking, funding, and oversight.

Top 3 Priorities

Lower Costs

Commitment: Take on price gouging and make daily life more affordable.

  • Ban corporate price fixing and junk fees through federal enforcement.
  • Cap insulin and prescription prices under Medicare and extend those savings to all families.
  • Increase federal investment in housing and childcare so families have affordable options.

Healthcare You Can Use

Commitment: Lower costs and expand access so care is there when you need it.

  • Protect Medicare and expand subsidies so more families can stay covered.
  • Ban surprise medical bills and cap out‑of‑pocket costs.
  • Increase federal funding for mental health and addiction treatment.

End Corruption and Special Interests

Commitment: No corporate PAC money. Ever. I answer only to the people of Washington’s 10th District.

  • Ban stock trading in Congress and strengthen ethics rules.
  • Outlaw corporate PAC and lobbyist donations to federal campaigns.
  • Protect Inspectors General and whistleblowers from political interference.
  • Require transparency by posting schedules and holding regular town halls.

Supporting Priorities

Housing You Can Afford

  • Increase national investment in affordable and workforce housing near jobs and schools.
  • Tie federal housing dollars to renter protections that prevent unfair rent hikes and evictions.
  • Expand tax credits and down payment help for first‑time homebuyers.

Strong Public Schools

  • Increase federal funding so states can reduce class sizes, expand special education, and hire more counselors.
  • Fund safer, modern school buildings and ventilation upgrades.
  • Expand career and technical education that connects students to good jobs.

Good Jobs and Unions

  • Expand apprenticeships and skills training with federal support.
  • Strengthen the right to organize by updating labor law.
  • Provide more capital and credit for small businesses.

Safer Communities

  • Fund violence prevention and youth programs through national grants.
  • Tie federal dollars to transparency and accountability in policing.
  • Expand mental health crisis response programs with federal support.

Clean Air and Water

  • Modernize the electric grid and expand clean energy with federal investment.
  • Protect rivers, coasts, and forests with stronger enforcement.
  • Fund household and business energy efficiency programs to lower bills.

Protect Rights and Freedom

  • Pass a national law to protect the right to abortion and contraception.
  • Restore strong voting rights protections and fair district maps.
  • Strengthen protections for LGBTQ+ people and enforce anti‑discrimination laws.
