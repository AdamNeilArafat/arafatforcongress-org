Bills exist. What’s missing is willpower.

WA-10 includes JBLM families, Port of Tacoma jobs, Olympia workers, I-5 commuters, and neighborhoods from Spanaway to South Tacoma. People here need representatives who move bills, not just message about them.

  • No corporate PAC money
  • Transparent and specific: bill numbers, votes, and receipts
  • Working-family standard: action, not press releases

Issue cards

Housing affordability

Lower rent pressure and accelerate housing supply near jobs and transit.

Healthcare costs

Expand access and lower out-of-pocket burdens for working families.

Worker pay and rights

Support wages and bargaining power that match the cost of living.

Anti-corruption

Strengthen ethics rules so public office serves voters first.

Baseline accountability: co-sponsorship

If an issue is urgent, co-sponsorship is the minimum standard. Leadership means more than statements: co-sponsor, organize support, and force a vote.
