The Plan

Lower costs, honest government, and healthcare for everyone. No corporate PAC money, no strings, just people building power together. This isn’t about ideology. It’s overdue modernization that protects our way of life and strengthens the middle class that keeps this country running.

Good policy starts with listening before legislating.

Top Priorities

Clear commitments you can hold me to.

If a bill doesn’t help working families in WA-10, it doesn’t make the schedule.

Lower Costs

  • Crack down on price fixing and corporate collusion that raise the cost of living.
  • Ban junk fees across all services and industries that hide the real cost of everyday life.
  • Expand affordable housing and childcare near where families live and work.

Healthcare You Can Use

  • Protect and strengthen Medicare and the ACA and close coverage gaps.
  • Enforce the No Surprises Act and expand caps on out-of-pocket costs.
  • Fund local mental health and addiction care so help is close by.
  • Begin the path toward universal healthcare that guarantees coverage for everyone in America.

The United States is the only one of 38 developed countries without universal healthcare. We spend about $12,555 per person each year, the most in the world, while millions still skip care because they cannot afford it. It is time to build an American model that guarantees coverage, keeps choice for patients and providers, and ends the waste and price games that drive up costs.

End Pay to Play Politics

  • No corporate PAC money, ever
  • Ban stock trading in Congress
  • Protect whistleblowers and Inspectors General

What I’ll Fight For

Real work that moves WA-10 forward.

Housing You Can Afford

  • Streamline zoning and permits for affordable and workforce housing.
  • Tie federal funds to fair renter protections and eviction due process.
  • Help first-time buyers get in the door with real down-payment support and a federal mortgage capped at 3 percent so working families can build wealth and stability.
  • Provide tax credits for ADUs and small starter home conversions.

Strong Public Schools

  • Boost Title I and IDEA so districts can lower class sizes and expand special education
  • Modern classrooms with safe air and water
  • Career and technical programs linked to local jobs

Good Jobs and Unions

  • Expand apprenticeships and skills training
  • Protect the right to organize
  • Increase capital access for small business

Safer Communities

  • Invest in youth programs and violence prevention
  • Transparency and accountability tied to federal dollars
  • Expand mental health crisis response
  • Increase social safety programs that help lift people out of poverty, which is proven to reduce crime and strengthen communities.

Clean Air and Water

  • Modernize the grid and speed clean energy
  • Protect rivers, coasts, and forests
  • Fund home and small business efficiency

Protect Rights and Freedom

  • Protect access to abortion and contraception
  • Restore voting rights and fair maps
  • Defend LGBTQ+ Americans from discrimination

I’m running to fight for people, not the powerful. If you’re tired of political games and want someone who understands what it costs to raise a family and make ends meet, this campaign is for you. I know what working people are up against, and I’m ready to stand up for us. If you’re working class and tired of politicians who talk big but never fight for you, vote for someone who will. Vote for me.
