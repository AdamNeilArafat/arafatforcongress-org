Lower Costs

  • Crack down on price fixing and corporate collusion.
  • Ban junk fees that hide real costs.
  • Expand affordable housing and childcare near where families live and work.

What I’ll fight for: Real cost relief that working families can feel in monthly budgets.

Healthcare You Can Use

  • Protect and strengthen Medicare and the ACA.
  • Enforce surprise billing protections and cap out-of-pocket burden.
  • Fund local mental health and addiction care.

What I’ll fight for: A path toward universal healthcare that guarantees coverage for everyone.

End Pay to Play Politics

  • No corporate PAC money.
  • Ban stock trading in Congress.
  • Protect whistleblowers and inspectors general.

What I’ll fight for: Transparent government that answers to voters first.

Housing You Can Afford

  • Streamline zoning and permits for affordable housing.
  • Support renter protections and due process.
  • Expand first-time buyer support.

What I’ll fight for: Practical pathways to housing stability and homeownership.
