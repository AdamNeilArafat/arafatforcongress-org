Lowering the Cost of Living

Problem: Families are getting squeezed by housing, groceries, childcare, and energy bills.

Plan:

  • Speed housing permits and build near transit.
  • Cut junk fees and price games at the register.
  • Expand childcare support so parents can work.
  • Help families lower energy bills with upgrades.
  • Protect Social Security you earned.

Result in WA-10: A fair shot to live and stay in our communities.

Healthcare You Can Use

Problem: Care is complex, expensive, and easy to lose when life shifts.

Plan:

  • Universal coverage with simple enrollment.
  • Primary-care first, no surprise bills.
  • Lower drug costs and hospital prices.
  • Protect veterans’ care and rural access.

Result in WA-10: Doctor first, paperwork last—so people actually get care.

Clean Campaigns & Anti-Corruption

Problem: Big donors and outside money tilt priorities away from WA-10.

Plan:

  • Ban corporate PAC money; disclose dark money.
  • Strengthen small-donor power and transparency.
  • Close lobbyist loopholes and end pay-to-play.

Result in WA-10: Decisions for neighbors, not national networks.

Fair Economy & Tax Commonsense

Problem: The rules reward those at the top while working families carry more of the load.

Plan:

  • Tax fairness that protects working- and middle-class families.
  • Support training and local jobs that actually pay.
  • Reward small business and made-here work.

Result in WA-10: A fair economy that grows from the middle out.
