Issues

These are the priorities Arafat will champion in Congress.

Economic Opportunity

Arafat will champion job creation and support small businesses. He believes in raising wages, strengthening workers’ rights and investing in job training so that everyone has a fair shot at prosperity.

Education

Every child deserves a world‑class education. Arafat will fight to fully fund our public schools, pay teachers a living wage, and make higher education and vocational training affordable and accessible.

Healthcare

No one should go broke because they get sick. Arafat supports expanding access to affordable healthcare, lowering prescription drug costs and protecting reproductive rights.

Environment

Arafat will work to preserve our natural resources and address climate change by investing in clean energy and green infrastructure, while protecting jobs and communities during the transition.

Veterans & Service Members

Our veterans and military families deserve respect and support. Arafat will secure funding for quality healthcare, housing and job programs for those who have served our country.
