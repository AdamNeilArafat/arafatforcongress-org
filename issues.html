Issues

We focus on what families feel every month: housing, healthcare, and a government that answers to voters, not lobbyists.

No new taxes on working families.

Lowering the Cost of Living

Families are being squeezed by housing prices, grocery bills, childcare, and utilities. We can ease that pressure with targeted steps.

  • Housing: Speed permits, support modular housing, and fix zoning barriers so affordable homes get built faster.
  • Groceries: Crack down on junk fees, block anti-competitive contracts, and enforce real competition so families pay less.
  • Childcare: Expand and refund the Child & Dependent Care Tax Credit so parents can actually afford care.
  • Utilities: Larger rebates for heat pumps, weatherization, and solar. Expand LIHEAP so no family is left in the cold.
  • Social Security: End tax on benefits and secure long-term solvency by lifting the cap on ultra-high earners.
  • Jobs: More apprenticeships, licensing reciprocity, and earn-while-you-learn training that lead to good pay.

Target

By 2028, reduce combined housing + childcare + utility costs by 15% for working families.

Funding

Close corporate tax loopholes and cut wasteful subsidies. No new taxes on working families.

Equity

Prioritize relief for rural families, seniors, and communities of color most impacted by rising costs.

Universal Healthcare

Healthcare is a right. No one should lose coverage because they changed jobs or missed a payment.

  • Automatic coverage: Everyone is enrolled from birth to retirement. No forms. No gaps.
  • Lower drug prices: Break patent abuses and expand drug price negotiations.
  • Mental health and clinics: Enforce parity and fund clinics in underserved rural and urban areas.
  • Healthy environment: Cut toxic air and water that drive illness and costs.

Target

Universal enrollment by 2030 and a 20% cut in out-of-pocket costs for working families.

Funding

Reallocate subsidies from private insurance middlemen and leverage bulk drug purchasing.

Equity

Focus on underserved groups: rural towns, veterans, seniors, and people with disabilities.

Ending Corruption & Special Interests

Democracy should never be for sale. Corporate PACs and lobbyists should not decide our future.

  • Ban corporate PAC money and bundling: Close every loophole.
  • Real-time transparency: Public, machine-readable reporting within 48 hours.
  • Small-donor power: Stronger matching that lifts grassroots support.
  • End dark money: Full disclosure for all election spending.
  • Teeth for the FEC: Real enforcement and real penalties.

Target

By 2026, pass a clean elections act that bans corporate PACs.

Funding

End lobbying tax breaks and center grassroots-funded campaigns.

Equity

Every voter should have equal weight, no matter income or ZIP code.
