Lowering the Cost of Living

Make housing and childcare attainable, groceries fair, and utilities affordable. Protect Social Security and support working families.

  • Housing: Faster digital permits, flexible zoning near jobs/transit, support modular builds. Housing Supply & Permitting Bill First-Time Homebuyer 3% Mortgage Bill
  • Groceries: Ban junk fees and MFNs; require clear fee/contract disclosures; enforce real competition. Fair Food & Grocery Competition Bill
  • Childcare: Make the CDCTC refundable; grow high-quality care slots. Affordable Child & Dependent Care
  • Utilities: Larger rebates for weatherization, heat pumps, and community solar; modernize LIHEAP access. Energy Savings & Lower Bills
  • Social Security: Stop taxes on routine benefits; ensure solvency with fair top-end contributions. Working Families Solvency Bill
  • Good jobs access: Licensing reciprocity, apprenticeships, earn-while-you-learn. Good Jobs & Skills Bill
Target

By 2028, reduce combined housing, childcare, and utility costs by 15% for working families.

Funding

Close corporate loopholes and eliminate waste—no new taxes on working families.

Equity

Prioritize relief for rural families, seniors, and communities of color facing the steepest costs.

Universal Healthcare

Coverage that follows you. Affordable drugs. Equal mental-health treatment. Primary care close to home.

  • Automatic coverage: A national baseline so no one falls through a gap.
  • Drug savings: Expand negotiation and stop patent abuses.
  • Parity & access: Enforce mental-health parity; grow clinics in underserved areas.
  • Healthy policy mix: Align food, water, air, and workplace standards to health outcomes.

Target

Enroll everyone by 2030 and lower out-of-pocket costs by 20% for working families.

Funding

Shift subsidies from private-insurance middlemen and use bulk drug pricing.

Equity

Prioritize rural areas, veterans, seniors, and people with disabilities.

End Corruption & Special Interests

Stop pay-to-play. Empower the people. Real-time transparency and real enforcement.

  • Ban corporate PACs & lobby bundling; tighten coordination rules.
  • People-first finance: vouchers and small donations at the center.
  • Near real-time disclosure: machine-readable, public, enforced.
  • Stop dark money: beneficial-owner and pass-through disclosure.
  • Modern FEC: timely decisions with meaningful penalties.

Tax Fairness & Good Jobs

Fair rules for business. Accountability for bad actors. Clear routes to good livelihoods.

  • Tax fairness: 15% minimum book-profit tax; curb offshoring; boost CTC/EITC; add a simple fairness credit. Tax Fairness & Relief Bill
  • Corporate responsibility: Penalties for wage theft, extreme pay gaps, repeat OSHA/EPA harm; credits for good actors; standardized disclosures. CSR Bill
  • Good jobs & skills: Licensing reciprocity, apprenticeships, community-college/employer alignment, training incentives. Good Jobs & Skills Bill
Target

Grow middle-income take-home pay by 5–7% by 2029 with tax fairness and training incentives.

Funding

Revenue from fair-tax rules and repeat-offender penalties, reinvested in workforce pipelines.

Equity

Direct training dollars to high-need communities; publish quarterly results.

How We’re Different

  • We reject corporate PAC money. Our campaign runs on small donors.
  • We fight to lower costs now with concrete steps and timelines.
  • We treat healthcare as a right, not a perk tied to an employer.

Roadmap

  2. Year 2: Expand childcare credits. Start apprenticeship and licensing reciprocity programs.
  3. Year 3: Roll out universal enrollment. Pass clean-elections reform that bans corporate PACs.
