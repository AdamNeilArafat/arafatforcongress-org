Lower Cost of Living

Families are squeezed. I will cut junk fees, stop price gouging, and put money back in your pocket.

Plan highlights
  • Ban hidden and junk fees on tickets, banking, and utilities.
  • Investigate and penalize price gouging by dominant firms.
  • Raise the federal minimum wage and protect overtime pay.
  • Expand the Child Tax Credit and Earned Income Tax Credit.
  • Lower energy bills with home efficiency rebates.

Healthcare for All

No one should go bankrupt because they got sick. Healthcare is a right, not a privilege.

Plan highlights
  • Move to universal coverage and protect Medicare and Medicaid.
  • Let Medicare negotiate more drug prices and cap insulin at $35.
  • End surprise billing. Wipe out predatory medical debt practices.
  • Expand mental health and addiction treatment access.

Housing You Can Afford

We need more homes, faster approvals, and help for renters and first-time buyers.

Plan highlights
  • Boost building near jobs and transit with zoning and financing support.
  • Increase tax credits for affordable and workforce housing.
  • Stabilize rent and stop price-fixing and junk fees.
  • Down payment help for first-time buyers and veterans.
  • Support proven homelessness solutions that keep people housed.

Jobs and Small Business

Grow local jobs. Back small business. Reward work, not just wealth.

Plan highlights
  • Expand small business loans and cut red tape that blocks startups.
  • Buy American for public projects and keep work local.
  • Invest in trades, apprenticeships, and community colleges.
  • Close loopholes that let giants crush local competitors.

Veterans and Military Families

I served. I know the cost. We owe veterans timely care and real support.

Plan highlights
  • Fully fund VA staffing and cut the wait to see a doctor.
  • Expand mental health care, TBI care, and family support.
  • Protect GI Bill benefits and speed up claims decisions.
  • End veteran homelessness with housing plus services.

Public Safety

Safe communities need good policing, strong prevention, and real accountability.

Plan highlights
  • Fund local departments for training, staffing, and modern equipment.
  • Back proven violence prevention and youth programs.
  • Crack down on fentanyl trafficking and support treatment.
  • Require transparency and accountability to rebuild trust.

Education and Childcare

Strong schools and affordable childcare let families work and kids thrive.

Plan highlights
  • Increase funding for K–12, CTE, and special education.
  • Lower childcare costs with provider grants and tax credits.
  • Make community college more affordable and expand apprenticeships.
  • Protect school meals and mental health supports for students.

Infrastructure and Clean Energy

Fix what we use every day. Create jobs. Cut energy bills. Prepare for storms and fire.

Plan highlights
  • Repair roads, bridges, water systems, and transit in our district.
  • Expand broadband to every home and small business.
  • Back clean power, grid upgrades, and home efficiency rebates.
  • Protect forests and coasts. Fund disaster readiness.

Democracy and Money Out

I will not take corporate PAC money. Public service means serving the public.

Plan highlights
  • Ban corporate PAC money and dark money in federal elections.
  • Require real-time disclosure of large donations and ads.
  • Protect vote by mail and early voting. Stop voter suppression.
  • End partisan gerrymandering and tighten ethics rules.

Rights and Freedoms

Freedom means control over your own life and future. Government should protect that freedom.

Plan highlights
  • Protect reproductive freedom and access to care.
  • Pass the Equality Act and defend civil rights.
  • Give dreamers a path and fix legal immigration backlogs.
  • Defend freedom of speech and religion for all.
