Where I Stand

Lower costs, honest government, real results for working families in WA-10. No corporate PAC money, no strings, just neighbors building power together.

Top Priorities

Clear commitments you can hold me to.

Lower Costs

  • Crack down on price fixing and junk fees
  • Cap insulin and essential drug prices
  • Increase housing and childcare supply where families live and work

Healthcare You Can Use

  • Protect Medicare and ACA coverage
  • Ban surprise billing and cap out of pocket costs
  • Fund mental health and addiction care

End Pay to Play Politics

  • No corporate PAC money, ever
  • Ban stock trading in Congress
  • Protect whistleblowers and Inspectors General

What I’ll Fight For

Real work that moves WA-10 forward.

Housing You Can Afford

  • Build affordable and workforce homes near jobs and schools
  • Tie federal funds to fair renter protections
  • Help first time buyers and fair lending

Strong Public Schools

  • Boost Title I and IDEA so districts can lower class sizes and expand special education
  • Modern classrooms with safe air and water
  • Career and technical programs linked to local jobs

Good Jobs and Unions

  • Expand apprenticeships and skills training
  • Protect the right to organize
  • Increase capital access for small business

Safer Communities

  • Invest in youth programs and violence prevention
  • Transparency and accountability tied to federal dollars
  • Expand mental health crisis response

Clean Air and Water

  • Modernize the grid and speed clean energy
  • Protect rivers, coasts, and forests
  • Fund home and small business efficiency

Protect Rights and Freedom

  • Protect access to abortion and contraception
  • Restore voting rights and fair maps
  • Defend LGBTQ+ Americans from discrimination
I’m running to fight for people, not the powerful. If you’re tired of political games and want someone who understands what it costs to raise a family and make ends meet, this campaign is for you. I know what working people are up against, and I’m ready to stand up for us. If you’re working class and tired of politicians who talk big but never fight for you, vote for someone who will. Vote for me.
Adam Arafat, WA-10
