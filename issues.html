Issues & Plans

Concrete actions to help working families in WA-10

Lowering the Cost of Living

Housing, groceries, childcare, energy. Everything keeps rising except paychecks. Families deserve relief.

How I will do it

  • Ban price gouging and crack down on corporate consolidation that inflates prices.
  • Build more affordable housing and speed up permits for local builders.
  • Cap essential drug costs and rein in hospital monopolies.

Universal Healthcare

No family should lose a home because someone got sick. Health care must be affordable and accessible.

How I will do it

  • Universal coverage with simple benefits and automatic enrollment.
  • Negotiate lower drug prices and end patent gaming.
  • Invest in primary care, mental health, and rural access.

Fair Taxation

Close loopholes so working families do not subsidize the largest corporations.

How I will do it

  • Minimum effective tax for large corporations.
  • End offshore profit shifting and pass-through gimmicks.
  • Targeted relief for families and small businesses.

No Special Interests or Lobbyists

Politics should serve people, not donors. I will not take corporate PAC money.

How I will do it

  • Ban members from trading individual stocks while in office.
  • Disclose meetings with lobbyists and publish calendars.
  • Post budgets and earmarks in plain English.

Corporate Accountability

Good jobs and safe workplaces should be the norm, not the exception.

How I will do it

  • Hold violators accountable for wage theft and unsafe conditions.
  • Reward companies that create living-wage jobs here at home.
  • Support the right to organize and bargain for fair contracts.

Support Farmers and Fair Trade

Keep agriculture strong and trade fair for workers and small producers.

How I will do it

  • Invest in local processors and cold storage to cut costs.
  • Protect family farms from predatory contracts.
  • Enforce labor and environmental standards in trade deals.

Climate Action Now

Protect what we love and create thousands of clean energy jobs.

How I will do it

  • Scale up wind, solar, and transmission while cutting red tape.
  • Fund home weatherization and heat pumps to lower bills.
  • Hold major polluters accountable for the true costs of pollution.
