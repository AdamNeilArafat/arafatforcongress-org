Meet Adam

Born in The Woodlands, Texas, and the son of a Palestinian immigrant, Adam served 20 years in the military, including 12 years in Europe and Asia. After meeting his wife in Tacoma, Washington became home. They’re raising two boys who attend public school in District 10, where his wife volunteers with the PTA.

After the military, Adam kept serving through county projects that improve everyday life. He put himself through college while working full time, earning a bachelor’s and a master’s degree, along with professional certificates, awards, and commendations.

Experience and service

As a veteran and project manager, Adam values leadership and accountability. Living in Europe and Asia showed what’s possible when healthcare is guaranteed, education is accessible, and food is affordable and healthy. Here at home he’s partnered with nonprofits and labor to support good-paying jobs and safe workplaces.

Why it matters

  • Fairness and justice for every person
  • Investing in our kids and our public schools
  • Affordable, accessible healthcare for every family
  • Protecting our environment for the next generation

Our kids deserve a future, not a system stuck in place. Change starts with leaders rooted in our communities, not in donor lists.
