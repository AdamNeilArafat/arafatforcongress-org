Meet Adam

I'm not a career politician. I'm a dad, a veteran, and a public servant who’s seen how hard families in WA-10 work to make ends meet. I know the frustration of watching government forget who it’s supposed to serve. That’s why I’m running to bring accountability and common sense back to Congress.

From Service to Community

Adam served over 20 years in aviation and emergency operations, leading teams and solving problems under pressure. Today, he manages public infrastructure projects that keep our communities safe and running. He brings the same mindset to public office: get the job done and be accountable for the results.

Rooted in WA-10

Adam and his family live in Spanaway. They face the same challenges as everyone else, rising costs, crowded schools, and uncertainty about the future. That’s what drives this campaign: real life experience, not political ambition.

What I Believe

Honesty

People deserve leaders who tell the truth, even when it’s hard.

Accountability

Public office is a responsibility, not a privilege. Every decision should stand up to public scrutiny.

Service

Government should work for the people who pay for it, not for special interests.

Action

Talk doesn’t fix problems. Focus, follow-through, and real results do.

I’m running to make government serve people again. We can build a future where families can afford to live, work, and thrive in WA-10.
Adam Arafat, WA-10
