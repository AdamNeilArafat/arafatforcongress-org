Meet Adam

Who he is

Adam Arafat is a father, a veteran, and a public servant who knows what it takes to keep families steady. He is running because WA-10 deserves a representative who listens first and answers to the community.

Background & service

Adam spent more than 20 years in aviation and emergency operations, then moved into public infrastructure work that communities rely on every day. He treats public office the same way: do the work, own the outcome, and remember who you serve.

What this campaign means

This campaign is about practical results for working families: lower costs, stronger healthcare, good jobs, and accountability in government.

Values and principles

  • Honesty: Straight answers. No spin.
  • Accountability: Public decisions should stand public scrutiny.
  • Service: Government should serve people, not special interests.
  • Action: Problems are solved through persistent work, not speeches.

Quick facts

  • PAC-free campaign
