Meet Adam

Born in Louisiana and raised in Texas, Adam is the son of a Palestinian immigrant. He served 20 years in the military, including 12 years in Europe and Asia. After meeting his wife in Tacoma, Washington became home. They are raising two boys who attend public school in District 10, where his wife volunteers with the PTA.

After the military, Adam kept serving through county projects that improve everyday life. He put himself through college while working full time, earning a bachelor’s and a master’s degree, along with professional certificates, awards, and commendations.

Experience and service

As a veteran and project manager, Adam values leadership and accountability. Living in Europe and Asia showed what is possible when healthcare is guaranteed, education is accessible, and food is affordable and healthy. Here at home, he has volunteered with nonprofits and labor groups to support good-paying jobs and safe workplaces.

Values — trust you can measure

Transparency

Publish calendars, report every dollar in near real time, and keep doors open to the public. This ties directly to No Special Interests.

Fairness

End loopholes and special treatment so workers and small businesses are not subsidizing corporate giants. See Fair Taxation and Corporate Accountability.

Responsibility to families

Lower the cost of living and target help where costs are highest. See Cost of Living.

Dignity in care

Healthcare is a basic human right. See Universal Healthcare.

Our goal is simple. Make life better now and secure a brighter future for our kids and grandkids.
