Meet Adam

I’m a father, a veteran, and a public servant who knows what it takes to keep families steady. Too often government forgets the people it’s supposed to protect. I’m running because WA-10 deserves a representative who listens first and answers to the community, not to wealthy donors or outside interests.

I’ve lived the same math everyone else in WA-10 lives: mortgages, childcare, and traffic on I-5.

From Service to Community

Adam spent more than 20 years in aviation and emergency operations, leading crews, solving tough problems, and keeping people safe. Now he oversees public infrastructure projects that communities rely on every day. He treats public office the same way he treated every mission: do the work, own the outcome, and never forget who you serve.

Rooted in WA-10

Adam and his family live in Spanaway, facing the same pressures everyone else feels: rising costs, packed schools, and a future that gets harder to plan for. That real-life grounding is what drives this campaign. He isn’t chasing a career. He’s fighting for the district he calls home.

What I Believe

Government works when the people writing the laws live under the laws they write.

Honesty

People deserve straight answers. No spin, no excuses.

Accountability

Holding this job means owning every decision. If it can’t stand up to public scrutiny, it shouldn’t happen.

Service

Government should put the people who fund it first, not the special interests trying to steer it.

Action

Problems don’t get solved by speeches. They get solved by focus, persistence, and results.

I’m running to make government serve people again. We can build a future where families can afford to live, work, and thrive in WA-10.
Adam Arafat, WA-10
