Meet Adam Arafat

Trust is built, not bought. I’m a dad, veteran, and public servant running to make Congress work for the people of WA-10 — lowering costs, cleaning up government, and fighting for working families.

Chip In Volunteer Read the Issues

Rooted in Service, Work, and Family

Spanaway is home. I’m raising my kids here with my wife, tackling the same costs, commutes, and classroom challenges as every neighbor.

Service

Twenty years in the Army leading aviation maintenance and airfield teams taught me discipline, accountability, and how to bring people home safe.

Work

Today I work in county public service delivering infrastructure projects on time and on budget so families have reliable roads, utilities, and services.

Family

As a dad and husband, I feel the pinch of higher groceries, rent, and childcare. Those lived experiences drive every decision I make.

What I’ve Done

Army Veteran

I led aviation units, managed complex logistics, and trained teams to succeed under pressure. Mission-first leadership means planning ahead, communicating clearly, and never leaving people behind.

  • Coordinated maintenance schedules that kept aircraft mission-ready
  • Built diverse teams and mentored young service members
  • Delivered results even when resources were tight

Public Servant

In local government I manage capital projects that improve daily life — from safer intersections to resilient infrastructure.

  • Plan scope, schedule, and budget with full transparency
  • Coordinate contractors, engineers, and community stakeholders
  • Guard taxpayer dollars like every project is for my own family

What I’m Fighting For

Working families deserve real results: lower costs, stronger services, and a government that answers to you.

  • Lower costs: Stop price gouging, end junk fees, and put family budgets ahead of corporate profits.
  • Healthcare you can use: Expand coverage, cap prescription costs, and invest in mental health and addiction care.
  • Protect rights and freedoms: Safeguard voting rights, reproductive freedom, and equality for every neighbor.
  • A fair shot for every family: Fund great public schools, apprenticeships, and safe communities so everyone can thrive.
  • Clean government: Ban corporate PAC money, end congressional stock trading, and publish every vote, meeting, and dollar.

Accountability & Independence

I refuse corporate PAC and lobbyist money. That freedom lets me put neighbors first and demand sunlight on every decision in Washington, D.C.

My promise: no PAC checks, no backroom deals, full transparency you can verify.

Questions I Hear Most

Why are you running?

Because families in WA-10 deserve someone who understands rising costs and will fight every day to lower them. Government should work for ordinary people, not the powerful.

How will you get results in a divided Congress?

I’ve built coalitions my entire career. Set clear goals, listen first, find honest partners, and keep working until the job is done — that’s how you deliver.

What does independence mean to you?

It means owing nothing to special interests. Every vote, meeting, and dollar will be public so you always know who I’m fighting for.

I’m running to fight for people, not the powerful. If you’re tired of political games and want someone who understands what it costs to raise a family and make ends meet, this campaign is for you. I know what working people are up against, and I’m ready to stand up for us. If you’re working class and tired of politicians who talk big but never fight for you, vote for someone who will. Vote for me.
Adam Arafat, WA-10
