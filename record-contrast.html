WA‑10 deserves better than politics as usual. I’m not running for a title or a career. I’m running to fix what’s broken and make Congress work for the people who live and work here.

Representative Marilyn Strickland: Who Funds Her

Eighty-five percent of her campaign cash comes from outside WA-10. That means her priorities are shaped by people who don’t live here, don’t work here, and won’t feel the cost of her decisions.

Her record reflects those alliances:

  • Takes major funding from the pro-Israel lobby, including AIPAC-aligned donors, and other corporate PACs tied to Wall Street, pharmaceutical corporations, defense contractors, and telecom giants. OpenSecrets
  • Voted YES on H.R. 6090, the Antisemitism Awareness Act, a bill the ACLU and FIRE warn will chill campus speech by wrongly equating criticism of the Israeli government with antisemitism. Congress.gov ACLU
  • Opposed fair-share tax reforms that would make large corporations pay what they owe.
  • Ignored the rising cost of living while siding with the same companies driving prices up.

This isn’t an accident. It’s what happens when you answer to donors and lobbyists instead of the people who actually live in WA-10.

Why It Matters

WA‑10 is facing the highest costs in years for housing, healthcare, and groceries while wages stay flat. That didn’t happen by accident. It happened because leaders stopped planning for us and started protecting themselves. They had eight years to fix it. Instead, they worked for their pocketbooks and special interests. We can’t afford more excuses. We need results that put people first.
