What this means for WA-10

When committee money becomes a major share of campaign funding, voters should ask whether public priorities are getting crowded out by donor priorities.

Accountability starts with transparency, then action: track the funding, track the votes, and demand measurable results for working families.

Accountability is not a slogan. It’s a method.

What “fighting” means in Congress

Real accountability means using leverage to force action. It means recorded votes, timelines, and escalation when leadership stalls. Co-sponsoring a bill is the floor. Moving it is the job.

If a policy has broad support and never gets a vote, that’s not confusion. It’s a choice. WA-10 deserves a representative who treats results like the job, not a photo op.
