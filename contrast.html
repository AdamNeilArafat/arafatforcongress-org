Explanations

When most money comes from outside WA-10 and from PACs it creates incentives that do not always match local needs. Transparency lets voters decide who their member answers to.

Routine bipartisan votes are common. The test is whether a representative shows up and leads on tough issues like healthcare for all and housing affordability. That is the work I will do.
