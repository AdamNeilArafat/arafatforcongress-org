Notes and methods

Percentages use OpenSecrets style categories for geography and PAC share. Out-of-district means donations tied to ZIPs outside WA-10. PAC share includes corporate, trade, labor, and issue PACs. High-dollar donors refers to larger individual contributions in FEC itemized data. Estimates round to illustrate scale.

“AIPAC” refers to AIPAC PAC and aligned bundled individual giving reported in federal filings and trackers. Mention here signals alignment and incentives. It does not allege illegality or foreign money.

Bipartisan index scores reward cross party cosponsorship and votes which often center on uncontroversial items like defense reauthorizations or ceremonial measures. Leadership on hard fights shows up in initiating, organizing, and moving contested policy to the finish line.
