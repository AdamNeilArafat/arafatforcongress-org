Why It Matters

This election is about the future we want to leave our kids. Do we keep the same system where corporate money calls the shots, or do we finally choose leaders who put people first?

The incumbent has shown where her loyalties lie — to big donors and outside interests. That path keeps us stuck. Adam represents a different choice: a working-class voice, free from PAC control, focused on universal healthcare, affordable living, and rebuilding trust in government.

Choosing Adam isn’t just rejecting politics as usual — it’s investing in a future where integrity matters more than influence, and where Washington’s 10th District sets the example of people-powered change.
