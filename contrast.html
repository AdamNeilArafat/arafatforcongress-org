Why It Matters

Leadership choices have consequences. If funding comes from corporate PACs and national lobbying operations, those interests set the agenda. I fund this campaign with people, not PACs, so my priorities stay where they belong: healthcare you can afford, a lower cost of living, and a government that works for you.

The future of Washington’s 10th needs independent leadership that is free to say no to special interests and yes to families, workers, and small businesses. That is the difference on this ballot.
