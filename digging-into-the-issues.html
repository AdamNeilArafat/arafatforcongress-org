Top Issue #1

Lowering the Cost of Living

Make housing and childcare attainable, groceries fair, utilities affordable, and safeguard Social Security, with no new taxes on working families.

  • Housing supply & permitting: faster digital approvals, flexible zoning near jobs and transit, support modular builds.
    Housing Supply & Permitting Bill First-Time Homebuyer 3% Mortgage Bill
  • Groceries & fair markets: crack down on junk fees and MFNs, standardize fee and contract disclosures, strengthen regional competition.
    Fair Food & Grocery Competition Bill
  • Childcare you can find and afford: expand and refund the CDCTC, invest in quality seat supply.
    Affordable Child & Dependent Care
  • Lower monthly utility bills: bigger rebates for weatherization, heat pumps, and community solar; streamline LIHEAP.
    Energy Savings & Lower Bills
  • Protect Social Security checks: end benefit taxes for most retirees; fair top-end contributions; strengthen trust-fund solvency.
    Social Security — Working Families Solvency Bill
  • Open doors to work: right-size licensing, universal reciprocity, expand apprenticeships and paid earn-while-you-learn.
    Good Jobs & Skills Bill
Top Issue #2

Universal Healthcare

Cover everyone automatically, lower drug costs, treat mental health like health, and invest in primary care close to home.

  • Automatic coverage: a national benefits floor so no one falls through the cracks.
  • Lower drug prices: expand negotiation and stop patent gaming.
  • Parity & access: enforce mental-health parity; grow clinics and workforce where care is scarce.
  • Health in all policies: align food, water, air, and workplace standards to health goals.

Top Issue #3

End Corruption & Special Interests

Stop pay to play. Make elections people powered with real-time transparency and strong enforcement.

  • Ban corporate PAC and lobbyist bundling; tighten coordination rules.
  • Voter vouchers and small donations to center campaigns on people, not big money.
  • Near real-time, machine-readable reporting with strong penalties for violators.
  • Close dark-money loopholes with beneficial owner and pass-through disclosure.
  • Modernize the FEC for timely, enforceable decisions and referrals.
Clean Campaigns & Elections Bill Congressional Vote Justification Bill

Tax Fairness, CSR, and Good Jobs

Make the economy work for working people. Close loopholes at the top, hold chronic violators accountable, and open more paths to good-paying work.

  • Fair tax rules: 15% book-profit minimum; end offshoring games; expand CTC and EITC; simple fairness credit.
    Tax Fairness & Relief Bill
  • Corporate responsibility: surtax for wage theft, extreme pay gaps, repeat OSHA and EPA harm; credits for good actors; standardized disclosures.
    Corporate Responsibility (CSR) Bill
  • Good jobs & skills: licensing reciprocity; apprenticeships; community college and employer alignment; training incentives.
    Good Jobs & Skills Bill
